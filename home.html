About Us

A R T Leasing Limited is an entity under the category of Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) regulated by the Reserve Bank of India.

ART Leasing started operations and was incorporated in 1995 as a non-banking financial institution (NBFC) as ART Leasing Limited, a public limited company with the approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The branches of this company are functioning in most of the regions of Central Kerala and South Kerala. We provide financial services with a focus on the future of our customers. GOLD LOAN

A Gold Loan, often termed a Loan Against Gold, is a secured borrowing option where individuals pledge their gold ornaments or assets as security to access funds. The loan amount is calculated based on the purity, weight, and current market value of the gold, ensuring a fair and transparent valuation process.

At A R T Leasing Limited, we redefine gold loans with a seamless experience. Unlike conventional banking methods that involve exhaustive procedures and time-intensive formalities, our gold loan process is designed to be quick, efficient, and hassle-free. With minimal paperwork and instant disbursal, we ensure you receive the financial support you need without delay.

Our gold loans feature highly competitive interest rates, positioning us as a trusted partner for managing short-term financial needs. Repayment is straightforward, covering the principal and interest, with no hidden charges or complexities.

A R T Leasing Limited takes pride in offering a secure, reliable, and customer-centric approach to gold loans, enabling individuals to unlock the potential of their gold and achieve their financial aspirations with ease.

BUSINESS LOAN

Business Loan is a financial service through which businesses obtain finance for their business needs. It can be used by small, medium and large enterprises for their growth, efficiency and essential expenses.

Conditions for availing business loan- When funds are needed to start new businesses, For opening new branches or purchasing equipment, For daily expenses, salaries and purchase of stock, For promotional activities and advertising.

Advantages of Business Loan- Provides emergency funding, Provides affordable support for business development, Growth potential for small businesses and startups.

A R T Leasing Limited, understand our customers and provide them with easy, quick and perfect solutions for their requirements.Business Loans can be availed when an entity has an immediate requirement of funds related to business, with very minimal documentation, processing time and with immediate disbursal to customer business bank account.
